Project Report

House Party Finder

Problem statement

Canada has a diverse community and few countries in the world have larger porportion of immigrants than Canada. By 2017, around 22% of population in Canada will be foreign born. It is becoming increasing hard for people to make friends and socialize. Facebook and other social media networks allow us to keep in touch with our friends but the goal of social media sites is not to help us make new friends. House Party Finder will allow hosts to advertise their house parties while people who want to socialize will be able to find house parties. Unlike club events, there is no platform to advertise house parties.

The solution

via house party finder website, party host will be able advertise their parties, upload pictures and live stream their parties. Users will be able to search for parties in their area and rate the parties. Parties with very high ratings will be showcased under 'featured parties' on homepage to give them more exposure.
Using house party finder, people will be able to interact with others with similar interests and make new friends in the same city.

Description of what has been done

  • users are able to register and login into their accounts
  • party hosts can upload information about parties
  • users can search parties
  • hosts can live stream their parties
  • users can see similar parties to the one they are looking at
  • addresses are verified with Google API
  • user can rate parties

Performance testing

  • tried using text compression to make the page size smaller to load the pages faster.

Actions to secure the website

  • bcrypt hashing was used for passwords
  • users are restricted to access certain pages that they do not own
  • google API address verfication

flaws and limitations

  • it is possible to hijack the stream of a user and we want to add a password to secure the stream (e.g.: http://127.0.0.1:8082/secret/)
